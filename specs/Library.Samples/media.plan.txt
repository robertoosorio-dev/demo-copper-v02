# Media Plan — Luminary Health, Q3 2025 (NA)

## Scope

Advertiser: Luminary Health (LMH). Markets: US (EN + ES). Flight: Jul 1 – Sep 30, 2025.
Objective: prospecting + retargeting for appointment bookings.

## Media Partners & Connectors

- Meta (Facebook + Instagram) — connector: Direct Publisher (walled garden). Click tracking: On.
- DV360 — connector: Google DV360 (served). Click tracking: On.
- Yahoo DSP — connector: Yahoo DSP 3PAS (served). Click tracking: On.
